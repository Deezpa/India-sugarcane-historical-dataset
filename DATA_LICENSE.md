## Responsible Use and Provenance

Users of the dataset must:
- Preserve all provenance-related fields (e.g., data source, confidence level, publication year)

## Canonical Reference

The **canonical, citable version** of the dataset is the version deposited in **Harvard Dataverse** under a persistent DOI.

GitHub serves as an operational and reproducibility mirror; the Dataverse record is the authoritative archival source for citation and audit purposes.
